Tongue twisters are sentences made of specially selected words, which are used for spelling trainings. Also, it is easier and more interesting to remember new words. 

Tongue twisters are not only the fun, game or entertainment, they carry an important function: development of auditory attention, help the child to learn the pronunciation of sounds and words neatly, to acquire the right diction, to get out of speech abnormality, which greatly complicate the communication and understanding of interlocutors’ words.

Make up your own tongue twister to train the [s, θ, ∫, t∫, z] sounds. You may use English-Russian dictionary to choose the words beginning with [s, θ, ∫, t∫, z].
