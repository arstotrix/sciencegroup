Tongue-twisters are used to develop a correct pronounciation and sometimes for entertainment purposes.
They contain words with several same letters, which make it hard to pronounce fast and without mistakes.
Practice is important in every field of work, so after some exercises a person will be able to pronunce even an unknown sentence without making mistakes.
Tongue-twisters are engaging and can stimulate pupil's ambitions to do it correctly.
Including tongue-twisters in language programs of primary school may result in forming a fluent speech and increase it's pace which will be very helpful in the future.
