A child who is studying in a primary school is likely to know more than a score of the most popular tongue twisters. The fact is that tongue twisters are included in some educating programs. However, do kids really need it? Doubtless they do. 
Firstly, tongue twisters are a way to help children improve their language development. Speech becomes more flexible and sophisticated.  Secondly, it can be used to help reduce the prominence of a foreign accent. Thus, tongue twisters with its playing aspects help overcome serious problems. 
Nowadays, not only pupils considered to remember some of tongue twisters.  For instance, it is a part of a job for people who work on television or radio. It does mean that exercises like this could be helpful even in adulthood - another great benefit of using tongue twisters.
