There are lots of tongue twisters in English. These are sentences made by specially selected words, which are used for spelling trainings. Also, it is easier and more interesting to remember new words. 

Tongue twisters are not only the fun, game or entertainment, they carry an important function: developing auditory attention, help the child to learn the pronunciation of sounds and words neatly, to acquire the right diction, to get out of speech abnormality, which greatly complicate the communication and understanding the words of interlocutor
