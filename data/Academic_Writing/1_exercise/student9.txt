Tongue twisters are supposed to show the difficulty of the pronunciation and they are used to help students to teach an articulation.  Tongue twisters are exercises, which people use to develop their speech, therefore they are essential in a primary school. Scholars find them funny and laughable, but in the same time they study to have elocution. Tongue twisters are also used in medicine in order to get rid of a speech abnormality, in politics and media to get legible pronunciation.
