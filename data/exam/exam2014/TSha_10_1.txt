The graph shows the proportion of the population aged 65 and over in three countries such as Japan, Sweden and USA. The diagram describe all changes of population in these countries between 1940 and 2040.
In Japan population aged 65 and over was about 5% in 1940. Then it declined to 3% by 1960 and stabilize by 2000. Between 2000 and 2040 we can see rapid growth of population aged 65 and over from 5% to almost 30 %. In Sweden this number was about 7% in 1940. Then we can see that amount of people aged 65 and over rised up by 1980 to 14% . Moreover, we can notice fast growth in this number to 25% by the end of the period.
In USA the number of people aged 65 and over isn't more than 15% throughout the period between 1940 to 2020. But from 2020 to 2040 it increases to 23%.
By the way, we can see that the number of people aged 65 and over tend to rise in all countries that was shown.
