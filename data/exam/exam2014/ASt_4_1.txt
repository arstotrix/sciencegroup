The graph demonstrates the percentage of population since age 65 in years 1940 and 2040 in three various countries. As an overall trend, it can be clearly seen that quantity of old people increase.
In 1940 the proportion of population aged 65 and over in Japan have only 5%. Then, after 20 years , it dresses to approximately 3% and remain stable through many years. After that it begin to grow and in 2030 there is a sharp increase from 10% to 25% and then in 2040 it is on the level of 27%. Other countries have the same line.
