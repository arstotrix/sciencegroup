The chart illustrates the amount of children, which are do not have an opportunity to get education in primary school from 2000 by gender and areas.
From the first glance, it is clear that Africa took the first place in the amount of children, which cannot be educated in primary schools. The interesting fact, that the propotion of boys and girls is aproximately equal, everywhere, exept South Asia in 2000. The number of girls as twice as bigger than boys (21,6 and 11,1). Moreover, South Asia had the smallest number of uneducated children in 2012. That was only 10 millions.
Overall, it is crussialy important that the number of children without access to primary education was decreasing per years. However, Africa in 2012 still has the biggest amount, which is equal to number of children in South Asia in 2000. However, the main trend is positive.
