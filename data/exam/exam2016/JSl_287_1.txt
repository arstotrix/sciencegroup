This bar chart illustrates the amount of time spending on sport activities by gender by different age groups. In the group of people of 16-24 years old and over 75 men spend the amount of time for sport activities twice higher than women (282,1 minutes/ 114,1 minutes and 29,2 minutes/10 minutes respectively). The 55-64 group is the unique one in which women waste more time for sport and exercises than men and it consists 52,7 minutes for men and 55,4 minutes for women. In all of other age groups the time spending for sport activities by men is higher than for women but the differences are not consist more the respective amount of time wasting by women in such groups. Overall, the most noticeable largest differences in amount of time spending by genders are in group of 16-24, over 75 and 55-64.
