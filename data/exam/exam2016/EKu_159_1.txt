The diagram given illustrates the number of Facebook users and how this number was changing from 2012 to 2013. The chart also explains the reasons of using this social network.  As it can be seen, the number of people that used Facebook in March 2013 with the help of desktop slightly decreased in comparision with the next year, while the number of those who used mobile devises to chat in Facebook increased approximately to 40 points.  Men's reasons are stable, the main are sharing photos and receiving updates. The most important for women is to share information as well. Seeing funny posts is more important for women, than for men. The least important for both sexes is to learn about ways to help others.
