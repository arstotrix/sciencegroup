The grath demonstrates the dynamic in the amount of boys and girls who have not an opportunity to get primary education from 2000 to 2012 around the world. Units are measured in millions of children.
Overall, there is a common tendency reducing the number of kids who cannot visit the primary school. The highest point is located in Africa and the lowest one is in rest of world (2000). After years, the minor of kids without primary education relocated to Asia (2012).
The great change happened in Asia. The amount of children decline from approximately 33 to exactly 10. The same dynamic is in Africa and in other part of world but numbers are not as big.
Moreover, boys who have not access to primary school are more than girls around the world.
