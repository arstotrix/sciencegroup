The given chart depicts how the number of children who do not have access to primary education changes over the time. Africa, South Asia and Rest of World are compared from 2000 to 2012. The key factors are region and gender.

Generally, african countries occupy the leading position by the quantity of boys and girls who are not able to get education. The most dramatical change was in South Asia where the number of kids without access to primary education was reduced in three times.
