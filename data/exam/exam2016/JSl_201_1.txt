The charts below give information about differences in the use of Facebook between 2012 and 2013 and main aspects of using it among 2 sexes. From the first chart it is clearly seen that users preferred visiting this site from the desktop to using it from the mobile. However, the number of people using Facebook from computers decreased from 150 to approximately 140 millions, when the quantity of mobile users increased significantly due the period. On the second chart it can be noticed that for both sexes the main reason of using this social networking service is sharing photos and videos. The equal percentage of men and women visit Facebook for receiving updates. Just 95 and 36 per cents of women and men respectively use this site for learning about ways to help others for each reason the number of women is equal or greater than the percentage of men.
