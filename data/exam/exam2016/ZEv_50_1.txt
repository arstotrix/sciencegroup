The chart illustrates the statistics of children without access to primary education during the period from 2000 to 2012 by gender and region.

The biggest number of children without primary  education was in Africa in 2000. Almost 45 millions african children did not have opportunity to get education, 20 millions of them are boys. The same levels have Africa in 2012 and South Asia in 2000, however the percentage of girls without education bigger. for 12 years number of children without access to primary school in Asia strongly reduced to 10 millions. Moreover, number of girls less than number of boys. In Rest of World level of children without access to primary education was almost 25 millions, major of them are girls with 12,8 millins. Up to 2012 amount of children without primary education reduced, and there was almost equality in comparison of boys and girls.

Since 2000 to 2012 the common number of children have been reduced in all regions.
