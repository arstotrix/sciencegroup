The graph provides information about the difference in time spent doing sport by man ad woman of different ages from 16 to over 75 in England in 2012. Overall, man spent more time doing sport from woman. Young people is more likely to do sport than olders. Almost all man of all ages dedicate more time doing sport than woman. However only in group of 55-64 the time spent doing sport by woman is higher than time spent by man. The significant difference between man and woman is in a group of 16-24 (282,1 and 114,1 respectively). General it is clear that despite the fact that man spent more time doing sport than woman. In group of 55-64 it is opposite situation.
