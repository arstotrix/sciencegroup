Today the problem of rising tempreture and pollution becomes more and more important. The whole world is starting to change it’s policy in the way for the planet saving. Air travel causes air pollution and the governments is interested in making an amount of air travel lower.
Some people say that this is a right policy because our planet is in a big danger. They also add that everyone must understand the importance of planet saving because people must give an opportunity for their children to live in the clean “green” planet, to breath a fresh air.
However, other people say it is not democratically right to forbitten the human to have an air travel because everyone must have an opportunity to move wherever and whenever he wants.
In my opinion, it is important to try to reach the golden middle. I think that it is quite real to have an air travel with the smaller harm for Earth by making an amount of travelling lower.
