The given charts reveal the data about tourists who visited the UK and about the UK citizens who made travels abroad in certain years, including the information about the most popular countries visited by them.

	The main trend is that the UK residents traveled to other countries more that people from abroad visited their country. The United Kingdom citizens prefered European countries to the USA and Asian, the most visited country by them was France, more that 10 millions visitors reached it in 1999.

	From 1979 to 1999 there was a dramatic increase in number of people from the UK who visited different countries. The same trend was represented by number foreigners who visited the UK, however the growth was more steady.

	Overall, there was an increase in number of tourists travelling to different countries during the period of 20 years, and most of the UK residents in 1999 went to France.
