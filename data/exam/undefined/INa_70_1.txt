The charts provide information about the different ages of the population of Yemen and Italy in 2000 and 2050.
   It is easily seen that the amount of people in the age of 15-59 will higher in Yemen in 2050 comparing to 2000. Also it is predicted that people in the age of 60 and more will remain approximately at the same level by 2050 in Yemen. But in Italy number of people in the age of 60+ will dramatically increase by 2050. Amount of people in the age of 15-59 in this country will fall from 61.6% to 46.2%. In Yemen the number of people in the age of 0-14 years will decrease as well as in Italy.
   Generally speaking, the charts show that the amount of people in the age of 60+ years has a tendency to increase in both countries. And the amount of people in the age of 0-14 years is predicted to fall by 2050 in both countries.
