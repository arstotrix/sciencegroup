The data represents the information about the qualifications of both females and males after graduating schools in 1999. According to the given information it is seen that there is a big correlation between women's and men's preferences. As it is shown a significant number of males have a skilled vocational diploma. By the contrast, this diploma is the least preferable one for women as only ten percentages have it. Bachelor's degree is popular both with males and females and it is given for nearly a half of people in Australia. It is claimed that a postgraduate diploma is not as important for females as for males. Twice as many men have it in Australia in comparison with women. So, we cannot say the same about an undergraduate diploma as it is mostly preferable with women. By comparison, only 35 percentages of men have it. To conclude, there is a significant different between genders, however, both of them are eager to get qualification.
