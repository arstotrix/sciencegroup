The diagram illustrates how many men and women in Australia held different types post-school qualifications in 1999. The most popular post-school qualification among men was the skilled vocational diploma - 90% of men received it in 1999. In contrast, only 10% of women got it. The most popular qualification among women in that year was the undergraduate diploma with 70% of women who received it. It is important to notice that likewise the skilled vocational diploma is the least popular among women the undergraduate diploma is the least popular among men. Approximately the same number of men and women held bachelor qualification and got bachelor's degree in 1999. Signicantly more men than women received the postgraduate diploma but not so many more men than women got master's degree in 1999. Overall, more men than women held different types of post-school qualifications in Australia in 1999.
