The graph illustrates global spartphone profits pf 4 companies: Apple, Samsung, Blackberry and HT between the years 2010 and 2015. As an overall trend, the percentage share of Apple is the highest in this market. The share of HTC profits stood at 9% in 2010. It climbed to 15% at 2011 and then decreased till 2015. Share of Blackberry profits fell rapidly between 2010 and 2012 and then more steadily, when it stood at approximetly at 54% in 2015. Profits of Samsung increased and reached a peak of 42% in 2013. Then profits dramatically declined. The share of Apple profits increased between 2010 and 2012, when it had 70%. After period of decrease in 2013 the share noticebly rose and achieved 91% in 2015. It can be clearly seen that the second company in share of smartphone profits in all countries was Blackberry in 2010 and Samsung in 2015.
