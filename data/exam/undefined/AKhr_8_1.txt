The graph illustrates the different studying degrees, which men and women in Australia had after school in 1999. Males got skilled vocational diploma more than females and by the males it reached its maximum of 90%. By the females the same qualification reached its maximum of about 12%. The different situation is on the qualifications of undergraduate diploma and bachelor's degree: there are more females than males. In qualification of undergraduate diploma by females it reached its maximum of 70% and by the males it reached its maximum of about 40%. What is more interesting, the difference between men and women in qualification of postgraduate degree is also big: for women it's 30% and for men it's 70%. Master's degree is connected more with males than females. On the chart the qualification of Master's degree for males is 60%.
