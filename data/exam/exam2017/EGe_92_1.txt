The graph presents data on print and electonic book consumption in 2014 with a forecast for 2018 in four different countries namely the USA, Germany, China and the UK.
As can be seen from the bar chart, the highest level of both types of books` selling is in the USA. Meanwhile, the least amount of money spent on the book market is in the UK. As for Germany and China, the situation on the market is approximately the same in both countries not only in 2014 but also in the forecast. It also should be mentioned, that in the USA and the UK more money is predicted to be spent on eBooks in 2018 than in 2014,although in Germany and China the changes from 2014 to 2018 are slightly noticeable. To sum up, it is thought that generally print books will be still more popular in 2018 than electronic ones.
