The graph shows the difference in the amount of unemployment rate from some world regions between 2014 and 2015
As can be seen from this bar-chart, North Africa has the highest unemployment rate (12,5%). It has remained on this level from 2014. The unemployment rate of Middle East has decreased from 11% to 9,6% respectively. What is more interesting, the amount of unemployment rate has risen from 7,5% to 7,9% in EU.
Furthermore, S. Asia has 3,9% in 2014 and continues to stay on this unemployment position in 2015. Besides, it is the lowest indicator of unemployment rate in this world. The unemployment rate of Latin America has dramatically declined from 6,8% to 5,8%. In other world the unemployment indicator has the same level. (5,9%)
Overall, almost all unemployment rates have remained on the same level between 2014 and 2015, except EU, Latin America and Middle East.
