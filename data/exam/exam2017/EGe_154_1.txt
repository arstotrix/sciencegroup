The given graph shows the data about the development of the print and eBook market in USA, Germany, China and UK in 2014 with a predicted numbers for 2018. It is clear that the print marker dropped from 2014, comparing with 2018, in contrast, eBook market will rose .

The numbers show that the most developed print market is located in USA, where 10.5 billion US dollars were received in 2014, and 7.5 billions US dollars will be received in 2018.Moreover the eBook market dramatically increased from 2014, so that in 2018 eBooks will be the most popular kind of books in USA, while in other countries eBook market will be developed much less quickly. In Germany the popularity of printed books in 2014 and 2018 will be the same, while eBook market will develop somehow. In China print market will be less popular in 2018 than it was in 2014, while the amount of electronic books will slightly increase. In the UK the same situation is noticeable - the amount of money from print market will decrease, while eBook market will develop following years.
All in all, it is obvious that electronic books will become more and more popular during these years, that situation can be explained by technological progress which is noticeable in all these countries
