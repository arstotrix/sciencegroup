The barchart demonstrates the percentage of online adults who use three social networks: Facebook, Instagram and LinkedIn. The data is provided by age groups.
According to the data, Facebook is the most popular social network in the US for all age groups. More than a half of adults from any age group use Facebook and almost 90 percent of adults aged 18 - 29 use this social network. Both Facebook and Instagram have significantly more users aged 18–29 and the percentage of users decreases for elderly age groups. Moreover, the percentage of Instagram users in the age group 65+ is significantly lower than this percentage for the remaining social networks.
In contrast, LinkedIn has on average similar percentage of users in all age groups: the numbers are between 22% and 31%. Moreover, LinkedIn has slightly more users in the age groups of 30 – 49 and 50 – 64 than Instagram, however the numbers are still quite lower, than the numbers compared with Facebook.
