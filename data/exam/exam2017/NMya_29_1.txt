The given diagrams represent the shares of online adults using a particular social network in the USA by age groups.
As one can see, the most popular social network in the USA from 3 given ones is Facebook because it has the biggest shares in all age groups. Facebook and Instagram have the youngest auditory as the percentage of age group 18 - 29 is the most prominent for these social networks. On the opposite LinkedIn users are in general older as the age groups 30 - 49 and 50 - 64 have the biggest shares.
Comparing Instagram and LinkedIn, one can conclude that among young users of age 18 - 29 LinkedIn is not as popular as Instagram because the percentage of online adults in Instagram is more than twice higher that for LinkedIn. Comparing the shares of elderly people who use Internet for each network one may mention that the most popular network in the USA for this group is Facebook.
