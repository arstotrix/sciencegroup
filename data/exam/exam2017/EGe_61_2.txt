Nowdays we can notice the tendency that large corporations seek to dislodge small businesses. The effects of such development stay unclear. Although, its advocates see more advantages than drawbacks.
On the one hand, giant companies representing a world-known brand are faceless. They try to manipulate their customers converting them in consumeristic belief. Moreover, speaking from the prospectives of employees working in such corporations, it is worth noting that the conditions do not correspond to standarts. Each single personality loses his significance and becomes a robot, that must pull his weight for a company to florish. Finally, such absorption of small businesses can lead to not national-oriented market, because large brands are considered to be globolized. So products will not be adapted for each culture any more.
On the other hand, giant companies provide people with a great number of work places.Also, they form the most part GDP of the country, because they are liable for mass production.
In conclusion, this essay emphasizes that it is hard to evaluate all pros and cons this process can triger. As far as I am concerned, I believe that both forms of business manifistations should take place.
