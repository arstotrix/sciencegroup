The chart below gives information about the average time spent doing sport and exercise, depending on people's age and gender, in England in 2012.
Firstly, it average, men spent more time on sport, than women. The only exception is the group of 55-64 years old men, whose average time spent doing exercise was 2,7 minutes less, comparing to the women of their age. The greatest gap between men and women was in the age of 16-24. Men of the age of 16-24 did sports two times longer.
