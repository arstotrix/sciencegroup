The bar charts illustrate the information about the differences in use of Facebook in the period of time from March 2012 to March 2013 and represent the data about the preferences of men and women. Overall, the changes were quite significant: the number of mobile version users grew and the number of the desktop site fans decreased, but still has lardger numbers than the mobile version.
As is presented in the first column chart, the number of people who use desktop version of this site is much higher than the number of those who prefer the mobile application. As can be seen in the second one, the percentage of women in all reasons for using Facebook is higher, except the one called "Receiving updates". For this bar the relative number of women is at the same level as is the percentage of men - 39%.
As observed in the chart of the reasons, there is no points where the relative number of men is bigger than 39%. The highest percentage is 54% of women who use Facebook mostly for sharing photos or videos. As for the chart of numbers of people using Facebook, only approximately 10 million of users had stopped using desktop site by March 2013 and the number of mobile application users had doubled by the same period of time.
