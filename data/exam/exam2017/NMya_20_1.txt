The given bar graph presents the information about the popularity of different social networks among adults in the USA who are using the Internet
The favourite network among all age groups is Facebook. 87 percent of people of 18 to 29 years old are registered. The number of percentage falls gradually to 73, 63 and 56 percent in subsecutive age groups.
The second social network in popularity among the youngest age group is Instagram. More than a half of active online users of 18 to 29 years old are keen on it. This number drops drastically for the second age group and decreases gradually for the third and forth group.
The third social network called LinkedIn is not used very often by people from 18 to 29 years old. The percentage of this group is lower than the percentage of all other groups. People of age 30 to 64 are equally interestend in the network
Young people are mostly interested in Instagram and Facebook, while older people are concentrated on Facebook and LinkedIn.
