The graph shows the situation in selected world regions in 2014 and 2015 and illustrates the level of unemployment rate in these areas.

The second place belongs to Middle East, but the level of unemployment had slight decrease from 11% to 9,6%. Moreover, the unemployment rate decreased in 1% in Latin America. The stable situation was in South Asia and Worldwide.
