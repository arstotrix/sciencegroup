The graphs given show some general information about uses of Facebook in 2012 and 2013 year. On the first diagram we can see, that usage of Facebook on mobile platforms became higher in 2013 year: from 50 millions uses to 90. Also desktop Facebook lowered from 150 millions in 2012 to 130 in 2013. In addition there are more uses of Facebook in 2013 in general. Second graph depicts reasons for using Facebook for men and women. For women the main reason for using Facebook is sharing photos and videos, and it is also one of main ones for men. That reason values are 54% for women and 39% for men. The second main Facebook using reason for men is receiving updates with 39% result, which is the same for both sex. Second best for females is 'seeing funny posts', with 43%. For men it is 35%, but only third place out of 4 reasons. In general women got more activites when using Facebook.
