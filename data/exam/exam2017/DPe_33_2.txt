Nowadays people like spending their free time on listening to music or watching films. Many of them are available in the Internet but almost all of them are illegal pirate copies. It is restricted to copy and share files by government rules therefore people have to be punished.
First of all, there are many international rules that don’t allow to download pirate versions of films and songs. With the development of technical devices and World Wide Net it has become very popular to get money from such network activity. People are against the law because they are not the authors of this or that film but they use it in an illegal way.
Secondly, people who copy original files without any permission decrease the income of musicians or film producers. It is clearly seen that authors loose a lot of money. Moreover, it takes a lot of time and efforts to make a film but not copying. The level of selled original discs sharply decreased when it became to be expanded. Individuals and groups of people work together and persuade companies to make films but pirate copies are able to lower their income very much. Furthermore, people got accustomed to watching pirate versions and they even believe that it is legal. No one wants to spend much money on discs therefore this problem can be called very serious in today’s world.
To sum up, it should be said that people have to stop downloading pirate copies of original versions if they don’t want to do something against the law. It prevents from increasing the budget of companies that make films, which everyone likes to enjoy. Unfortunately, punishment is the only strong method to stop it.
