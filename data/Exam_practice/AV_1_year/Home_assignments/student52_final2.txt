Report on herbal medicines
Introduction
This report investigates herbal medicines and considers whether this treatment is safe and effective.
The report gathered information from the following sources:
•	4,500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using the treatment. We received 2,815 completed surveys.
•	Articles from well-known medical journals were consulted.
Facts 
•	Plant extracts are used for these remedies.
•	Herbs have been used for thousands of years, so, it is an ancient remedy.
•	There are three types of herbal medicines: western, Chinese and Indian.
•	This kind of medicines is quite safe but users need to take care because there can be side effects from some herbs. Also, they can interact badly with other drugs.
•	Our members found herbs very effective for a wide range of illnesses but just over 55% said that they would recommend herbal medicine to their friends.
Summary
There are some herbal medicines which have a harmful effect on the body. However, there are many benefits from herbs as well.
Recommendations
•	It is advisable to use herbs with great care, so, you should get an advice from a herbalist.
•	Also, it is absolutely essential to tell your doctor if you are using herbal medicine.
•	You must make sure the herbal remedy is the correct product for you.
•	It could be a good idea for a herbalist to make a remedy specially for you.
•	You should not believe all the claims you read on the packaging of a herbal product.
