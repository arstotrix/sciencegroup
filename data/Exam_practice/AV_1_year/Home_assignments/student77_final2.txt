Report on herbal remedies
Introduction
This report investigates herbal medicine and considers whether the therapy is effective and safe.   
The report gathered information from the following sources:
•	4,500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using herbal remedies. We received 2,815 completed surveys.
•	Well-known medical journals were consulted.
Facts and findings
1.	Plant extracts are used in the process of production herbal medicines.
2.	Herbs are an ancient remedy. They have been used in treating many illnesses for thousands of years. 
3.	There are three types of herbal medicines: western, Chinese and Indian.
4.	Herbal remedies are considered to be quite safe. However, people who use them need to do it carefully as there are side effects from some herbs. What is very important is that these remedies can interact badly with other drugs.
5.	Our members used herbal medicines to treat a wide range of medical problems. 55% said that they would recommend herbal medicine to friends.
Conclusion
Although herbal medicines can cause harmful effect on the body, there are still a large number of benefits from herbs.
Recommendations
1.	You should consult your herbalist before using herbal remedies.
2.	It is absolutely essential to tell your doctor if you are using herbal medicine.
3.	You should not believe all the claims that you read on the packaging of a herbal product.
4.	It is advisable to make sure that the herbal remedy is the correct product for you. 
5.	It is a good idea for a herbalist to make a remedy especially for you.
