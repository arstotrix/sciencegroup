Report on Herbal medicines

Introduction
This report investigates herbal medicines and considers whether the medicines are effective and safe.
The report gathered information from the following sources:
•	4500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using the medicines. We received 2815 completed surveys.
•	Articles from leading medical journals were reviewed. 


Facts and findings

1.	Herbal medicine is the use of remedies made from plant extracts to treat illnesses. 
2.	Herbs are one of the most ancient remedies. They have been used for thousands of years.
3.	There are three types of herbal medicines: western, Chinese and Indian.
4.	People should be very careful when using herbs. There are many dangerous side effects. Moreover, they can interact badly with other drugs.
5.	Our members found herbs very effective for a wide range of illnesses. About 55% said that they would recommend the treatment to friends.

Conclusion

Scientific experts and users of the medicines are divided about their safety. Some say herbal medicines have a harmful effect on the body. Others, on the contrary, claim that they have many benefits as well.  

Recommendations

1.	You should get advice from a herbalist before using herbal medicines.
2.	It is absolutely essential to tell your doctor if you are using herbal medicine.
3.	It is also vital to make sure the herbal remedy is the correct product for you.
4.	It is a good idea to ask a herbalist to make a remedy specially for you.
5.	Do not believe all the claims you read on the packaging of a herbal product.
