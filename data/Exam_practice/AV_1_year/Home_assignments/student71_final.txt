Report on Herbal medicines

Introduction:

This report investigates Herbal medicines and considers whether the therapy is effective and safe.
The report gathered information from the following sources:

- 4.500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using the therapy. 2.815 of surveys were completed and sent back.
- Well-known medical journals were consulted.

Facts and findings:

1. The herbal medicines are made by use of plant extracts.
2. The herbs have been used as a remedy for thousands of years.
3. There are three types of herbal medicines: Western, Chinese and Indian one.
4. Our members found herbs very effective for a wide range of illnesses. Just over 55 percents said that they would recommend the treatment to friends.
5. Concerning safety, the users of herbal medicines need to take care. Some herbs can cause side effects or interact badly with other drugs. 
 
Conclusion:
Although it is considered that the Herbal medicine sometimes has a harmful effect on the body, scientific experts and users of the therapy agree that it has many benefits as well.

Recommendations:
1. Get advice consult a herbalist before using herbal medicines and try it with great care.
2. It is absolutely essential to tell your doctor if you are using herbal medicines.
3. Read carefully the instruction to make sure the herbal remedy is the correct product for you.
4. It may be a good idea for a herbalist to make a remedy specially for you.
5. It is not recommended to believe all the claims on the packaging of a herbal product.
