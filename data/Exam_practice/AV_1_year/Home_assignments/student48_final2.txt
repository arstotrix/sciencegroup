Research Report
Report on herbal remedies
Introduction
This report investigates herbal remedies and considers whether the methodic is effective and safe. 
The report gathered information from the following sources:
•	4,500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using the therapy. We received 2,815 completed surveys from the respondents.
•	Articles from well-known medical journals were consulted. 
Facts and findings
1.	Plant extracts are used for remedies. There are several types of them: seeds, roots, leaves, flowers. 
2.	Herbs are known as ancient remedy – they have been used for thousands of years (from the Paleolithic). 
3.	There are three types of medicines which differ greatly: western, Chinese, Indian
4.	Users need to take care about safety in usage herbal remedies. There are some side effects from them and could be unpredictable interactions with other drugs
5.	According to our research, herbs are found as very effective for a wide range of illnesses 
6.	55% of respondents prefer herbal remedies, so they would recommend this type of medicine to friends

Conclusion
In spite of the fact that some herbal remedies have a harmful effect on the body, there are meny benefits from herbs as well. 
Recommendations
•	Before starting usage of herbs consult a herbalist – this type of medicine should be used with great care
•	It’s absolutely essential to inform your doctor if you are using herbal medicine 
•	Make sure that the herbal remedy is the correct and appropriate product for your health
•	If you doubt about the effects caused by particular remedy on you ask your herbalist to make a remedy specially for you
•	Don’t believe all the claims you read on the packaging of a herbal product, something can be just an advertisement for good sales
