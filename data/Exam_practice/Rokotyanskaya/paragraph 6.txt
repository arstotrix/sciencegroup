It is broadly accepted, that poverty is one of the main problems in Russia and it has many different causes and consequences. Generally, poverty is a state of suffering from a lack of financial means. Problems like this are closely connected with nearly all aspects of people's life. On the one hand, high level of unemployment is one of the striking examples of poverty reasons. On the other hand, list of money shortage effects includes such issues as absence of accommodation and insufficient food or clothing of a large part of community. However most troubles connected with poverty, like quality of health care and educational standards, are both: sequences and causes at the same time, so that problems maintain and even increase poverty and themselves. Because of that and many other reasons, poverty is one of the largest obstacles for Russia on the way to prosperity.
