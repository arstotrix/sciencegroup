 Sports like badminton and tennis are quite similar but still differ in many ways. Both of the games were invented in the middle of 19th-century. The badminton game consists of two players on the one side of the net and two players on the other . While tennis can be played in either single or doubles format. System of earning points in badminton is quite common: to win you must earn 21 points and the game is considered over when one of the sides has won two rounds out of three. In tennis the score progresses in the following order: 15, 30, 40. Tennis matches are played in sets where once one of the players scored at least 6 points and beaten their opponent at least two times they are considered the winter of the game. Both sports are a lot of fun to play, they are followed all over the world with different success.
