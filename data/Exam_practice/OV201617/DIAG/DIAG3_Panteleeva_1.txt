The presented diagram shows the stages of producing different leather goods. First, animal skin has to be dry and that is why the first stage is drying. After that it is transported at the factory where the skin is prepared for making different goods. There the skin is washed and for this process water and lime are used. After having soaked the skin remains to be in the lime. The next stage is flattening. It is made automatically. After that the skin is immersed in one more liquid. Compered to the first liquid, this one is consisted of vegetable matter that is used instead of lime. This kind of liquid is called "tannin". Having soaked, the skin is polished automatically. Then it is transported at the factory where different leather goods, for example, shoes, bags, toys and others, are produced. This process shows how leather goods are produced.
