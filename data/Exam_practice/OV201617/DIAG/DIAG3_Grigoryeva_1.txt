The given diagram provides us with the information about the stages animal skin needs to go through in order to get prepared for leather goods are been produced from it.
The whole process begins with drying some animal skin. Then it is sent to the factory, where the skin is washed with the special mixture of water and lime. The next step is to let the lime juce soak in, and then flatter the skin with the help of special equipment. The flattered skin needs to soak in the substance called tannin (mixture of water and vegetable matter), and then it is ready for polishing. Polished skin is sent to another factory, which now can produce the wide variety of goods from the properly prepared leather.
