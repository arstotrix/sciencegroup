The diagram below gives information about a method producing leather goods.
The process starts with drying of an animal skin which should to be transported to a special factory. There the animal skin is been washing in a container full of water and lime. The next step of the skin processing is soaking in a container with lime only. As a result of these operations the animal skin is ready to be flattened by a special machine. However, it is not the end of water procedures. After flattening the animal skin should be soaked again and in this case in a container with tannin that includes water and vegetable matter. The last operation with the animal skin in this place is polishing, after what it looks like skin that people use in their ordinary life.
Next the animal skin is transported to a factory where skin goods are made and becomes shoes, bags, balls, etc to be used by people.
