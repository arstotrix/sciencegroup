The diagram below discribs a method of producing leather goods from drying animal skin to final product. 
The process of producing leather goods starts with drying animal skins before transporting it to the factory. There on factory it is being washed in water and lime solution. After that it is soaking in lime. Then, prepared skin is flattening between two cilinders in a machine. After being flattened the skin is soaking again in a liquid substance which is called tannin. Tannin is a vegitable matter solution. Then it goes to the last step of working on than kind of factory, the skin is conveyed to polishing machine where it will be finaly prepared to be produced into a final good.
After all of the work on skin preparing factory the leather is transporting to another factory where are produced final leather goods, for example it could be different kinds of shoes, bags, sport balls.
