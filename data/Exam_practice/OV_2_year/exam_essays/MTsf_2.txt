The picture in the task shows how frozen fish pies are producted . The manifacture  is devided in two stages: preparing potatoes and cooking fresh salmon. 

After potatoes are delivered they are cleaned, peeled, sliced, boiled and chilled. Thats how they are prepared to be stored and to be put in a pie. What about fish, after being delivered fresh salmon is cooked with lemon juice and salt. When skin and bones are removed from cooked fish it goes under inspection.

Prepared peas, sause, fish and potato are put in together and a fish pie is ready to be put in a microwaveable container and freezed.
