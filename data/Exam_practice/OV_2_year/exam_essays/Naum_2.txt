The diagram illustrates the process of manufacturing the frozen fish pies. It shows that there are two separete steps for preparing potatoes and fresh salmon.

Firstly, potatoes are brought to the factory. After that they are get cleaned and peeled, their peels go for waste disposal process. Sliced potatoes are boiled and chilled and go to the store. Second separated step is about preparing fresh salmon. It also get brought to the factory for fish pies. First process of adding lemon juice and salt lasts not long than 12 hours. Then skin and bones are removed by workers. An inspect checks out salmon. After that prepared peas and sauce are added to expented salmon. On the next phase potatoes get added to salmon and they are wrapped with a microwaveable container and freeze. Finally, they are ready for dispencing.
