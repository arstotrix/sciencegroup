   The graph gives information about spending money on petrol of British and Americans drivers with different income.
   It should be noticed that, in general, two countries have different tendencies: British follows the positive, while the USA - the negative. The poorest category of British drivers spends up to 3 % of their income on petrol, while in the USA expences of the same category starts from almost 4% and reaches more than 5%. 
In fact, graphics equals at the boundary between middle-income class and the richest people reaching 4 percents.
