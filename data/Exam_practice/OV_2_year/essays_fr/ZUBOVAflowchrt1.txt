The given chart shows the information about typical stages that the goods go through before being sold.  Also this diagram includes data about process how information is fed back to earlier stages of manufacturing process.
As we can see, the initial components are raw materials and manufactured components. First, they get into the storage. At the same time, we have to remember that production planning depends on product research and product’s design. But, design also depends on information from testing stage and market research. Information from market research is fed back to advertisement, which is one of the most significant parts of good sales.
After designers’ decision products undergo the most necessary stages involve assembly, inspection, testing and packaging. After a long process of preparation it’s time of dispatch -- the goods go on sale. In the end, the information of sales is fed back to designers and to production planning and everything is repeated again.
