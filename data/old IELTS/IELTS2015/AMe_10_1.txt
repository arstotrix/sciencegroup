The diagram illustrates the levels of post-school qualifications in Australia and proportion of people who held them in 1999.
	It is clear that for males main qualification is skilled vocational diploma (more than 90%) in contrast females got skilled vocational diploma mere 10%. For females main qualification is undergraduate diploma (70%). For males undergraduate diploma is lowest number (less than 40%)
	Other data give information about others qualifications (Bachelor’s degree, postgraduate diploma, master’s degree) We can see that in a rest qualifications lead postgraduate diploma (70% of males) and Bachelor’s degree (more than 50% of females)
