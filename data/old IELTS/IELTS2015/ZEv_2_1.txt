The charts provide data about travel to and from UK since 1979 to 1999 and about the most popular countries among UK residents in 1999.
According to graph there was increase in visits abroad by UK residents as well as in visits to the UK by overseas residents. A stable growth in visits can be noticed since 1979 to 1985 both for UK and overseas residents. A remarkable increase in travel took a place since 1985, reaching a peak in 1999 with more than 50 million visits by UK residents and 30 million visits by overseas. 
The barchart illustrates that the most popular country among UK citizens in 1999 was France with more than 10 million UK visitors, while Turkey was not as popular with less than 5 million visitors. 
Generally can be seen the tendency in travel increase among UK citizens as well as overseas.
