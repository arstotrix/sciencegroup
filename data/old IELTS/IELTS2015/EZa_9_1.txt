The line graph and the pie chart give us  information how people use electricity in own home and show the differences of demand for electricity in winter and summer.
According to the first graph, the main trend reveals that people tend to use more electricity during days in winter than in summer. In summer the demand for electricity rise from 15000 to 2000 units, while in winter the feature is flactuated. The demand is not stable so the using of electricity increase from 3500 to 45000 units.
According to the pie chart, people use more electricity for heating rooms and heating water. The electricity is used less for electric tools, radio or TV.
To sum up, the graph below shows that the demand for electricity increase in winter because the electricity is used for electric tools.
