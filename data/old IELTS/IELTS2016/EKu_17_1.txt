The first bar chart shows how many people used Facebook and which electronic devises they were using it from in March 2012 and in March 2013. In 2012 about 150 million users were browsing Facebook on their desktop computers. By 2013 this number dropped slightly to more than 130 million. On the other hand, the use of Facebook on mobile phones significantly increased during that period of time. It went from above 50 million users in 2012 to more than 90 million in 2013. The second chart illustrates the difference of reasons for using Facebook between men and women. The majority (54%) of women chose sharing photos and videos as one of the main reasons while only 39% of men chose the same option. Receiving updates was one of the main reasons for 39% of both men and women. Next there is seeing funny posts which was important for 43% of female users and for 35% of male. Lastly, learning about ways to help others was chosen by 35% of women while only 25% of men considered it important.
