The graph illustrates investment in renewable energy in the world between 2006 and 2013. Here we can see investment in developed countries, developing countries and world total investment. There are some trends in this graph. In 2011 there was a peak of investment in developed countries, and one year later, there was a peak in developing countries. Before 2012 the investment in developing countries growths. In total around all the world the investment growths from 2006 to 2011 but after that it goes down. 2013 is very interesting year, because this year difference of investment between developed and developing countries is smallest. The investment in developed countries is not very stability. There are two periods when the investment growth: the first period is between 2006 and 2008 and the second period is between 2009 and 2011. However, the investment in developing countries rises every year and only in 2013 is falling.
