The bar chart shows changes in the number of children without access to primary school education by gender and region from 2000 to 2012. It is clearly seen that the number of children without access to education commonly decreases. The most affected by the global development region was South Asia. The number of boys without education falls from 11,1 to 5,1 while the number of girls falls rapidly to 16,6 millions in there. It is also clear that the number of girls without education decreases faster than the number of boys.
At the moment of 2012 Africa has approximetely the same number of children without education as South Asia in 2000.
To sum up, global development is affecting every continent on Earth and it is very good. Nevertheless, Africa is increasing its development level really flow despite steady fall of the number of children without education.
