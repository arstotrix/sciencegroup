The timeline graph illustrates how income of Apple, Samsung, Blackberry and HTC have changed in last 5 years. The main trend of the graph is the leadership of Apple in whole period of time. It’s profits share rises from approximately 40% in 2010 to nearly 90% in 2015. While Apple’s share increases, the others’ are almost the same, like share of Samsung, which is nearly 15% both in the beginning and in the end of graph, or even decreases, like Blackberry’s and HTC’s shares.
