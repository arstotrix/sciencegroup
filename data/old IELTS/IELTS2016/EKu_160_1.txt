  The diagram below indicates changes in using of Facebook during 2012 to 2013  and the overriding reasons why males and females use it. It can be seen from the first bar chart that there is a decrease of about 1 million people in using the computer version of this social network &nbsp;from over a year-period. The number of users of Facebook's mobile version, in contrast, soared from approximately 60 million to about 98 milion people from 2012 to 2013.  The percentage of main reason for working with Facebook is provided by the second bar chart. It is shown that  for 1/2 of women it is opportunity to share their pictures and videos and only 39% of males use Facebook for this reason. The figure which presents the users who regular recieves updates is the same among both sexes. Funny posts and photos attracts about 50% of female users and more than 1/3 of men. For 35% of women and only a quarter of males it is essential to open Facebook because of tips about how to help friends and other people.  Overall, the main tendence in Facebook's statistics is an upturn of the average number of users durind 2012 to 2013, according to the first bar chart. In addition, it can be deduced that women are more interested in using Facebook than males.
