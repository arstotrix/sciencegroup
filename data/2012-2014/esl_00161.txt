The thesis examines the issue of authentic being of a person. Nowadays practical psychology deals with a growing number of clients facing the problem of choice. It is generally assumed that a person has a choice in every given situation, but the difficulty consists in making a choice specific to his/her individuality avoiding the social influence, impact of stereotypes and other outside factors. It means to make authentic choice. The basis of every choice is decision.  Authentic decision  -  decision made by a person according to particularity of his/her personality.
This paper focuses on researching of previous experience of authentic decisions as a primary tool for authentic  future -making.
