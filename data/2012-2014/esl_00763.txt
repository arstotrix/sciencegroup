A summary of points given in ex. 1 pp.20-21

In developed countries lifestyle of the youth does not include a lot of time for sports. Interestingly, it affects not only their health, but also their academic performance: the fitter pupils are more successful in school than their unfit peers. Physical activity also helps to deal with stress, to have better mood even can increase the crucial parts of brain. Moreover, going in for sports can have positive effects on mental health of aged people, as well as increase the capacity of children for multi-tasking (which means doing several things simultaneously).
