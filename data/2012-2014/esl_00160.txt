Despite  wide practical circulation of a problem of love addiction there is a small number of the researchesdevoted to this subject. The current paper suggests to consider a concrete case from therapeutic practice from the point of view of system family therapy which is connected withthe obsessive relations. The analysis of this case proves that the solution of the problem of love addiction lies in  change of the relations in a family, in  change of the main patterns of parental behavior and in  change of some representations and expectations about family life.
