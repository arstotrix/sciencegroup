The article analyses recent empirical evidence applying to the appeal and well-being effects of video games. The authors advance the problem of the need for research approaches that can extend scientific understanding of video game engagement. Therefore, unclear logical connection - why therefore? the main aim of the paper is to present a motivational model based on self-determination theory (Deci & Ryan, 2000; Ryan & Deci, 2000a) and make an attempt to explore the mechanisms through which video games influence human motivation and psychological wellbeing. This approach suggests that video game effects satisfy basic psychological needs for competence, autonomy, and relatedness. The authors prove that  introduced model may be effectively applied in  future study of video game engagement.
