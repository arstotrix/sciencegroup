The social-psychological mechanism of creation an individual image.
The concept of image is important for many practical areas: social institutions, marketing, advertising, management, etc. In a psychological sense, the essence of  the phenomenon of image-tracking links with the other general psychological  and socio-psychological concepts, particularly, in  the social perception. The problem of understanding the concept of image is that this phenomenon is not well defined. Different authors interpret it on the side of activity in which they operate.
Because the image is part of a complete image of the individual, it is important to  identify the mechanisms correctly that contribute to its formation. Due to combination of mechanisms described in the coursework the person forms his or her individuality to encourage the perception by society.
