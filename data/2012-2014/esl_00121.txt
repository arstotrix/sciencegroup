Introductory paragraph
My topic is "The alteration of a script as a result of reflected subjectivity of a significant other".  
 'Reflected subjectivity' - term  of  professor V.A. Petrovsky.   It means mental representation  one person in the psyche of another one. This representation considerably affects posing and solutioning problems.  
Problematics of the research is constituited by the fact, that people act different ways in different situations. It can be hypothetically explained by the phenomena of reflected subjectivity; namely, humans actualize script parental messages in the presence of different significant others.
