There is a great concurrency during companies nowadays. Each of them is interested in better producing. And one of the main questions is: “Does the success of the company depends on its employee’s happiness?
On the one hand, employees who are satisfied with the working condition and salary are happy in many cases, and as a result, they work harder, so the company has benefit. Besides, it’s profitable for the company when happy workers help one another in difficult situations. 
On the other hand, we should not think that everything depends only on workers’ psychological state. Much depends on the employers. If a director chooses right strategy of developing the company, it will have success by all means. Moreover, we should not forget that the employer ought to create suitable conditions for his worker to be happy.
