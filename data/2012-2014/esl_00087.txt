Implicit conceptions of love: gender aspect
Humanity has always been interested in the subject of love. Many philosophers and psychologists propose their own classifications and conceptions of this phenomenon. This subject becomes especially more complex in communication between men and women, due to differences in the psychologies of genders. The main goal of work is to research and describe the difference between males and females in their understanding of the concept of love.
