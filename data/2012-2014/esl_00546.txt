Fairmont Island is one of the resorts of Miami. According to the chart visitors enjoy heartful atmosphere there. People are welcoming and friendly there, and the scenery enhances a good impression of this corner of paradise and appeasement. Despite the wonderful landscape, the weather on Fairmont Island is not the most wonderful. 20 percent of visitors consider it one of the major disadvantages of Fairmont Island. The conditions are acceptable here - at least 11 percent of respondents think so. Local culture is quite developed, but leisure activities and entertainment are not organized (hardly any celebrities would get here to give a concert). Local cuisine differs the European. Perhaps it is too unusual for people from the continent, too spicy or something like that. In general, people, who are very particular about their diet, are dissatisfied with local food.
So, Fairmont Island is a beautiful place near the ocean with spectacular scenery, but it is not accessible to visitors with low incomes. If you are going to Fairmont Island, do not doubt that you will get a lot of positive emotions, but be ready for food, culture and people are very different and can be seem to be exotic.
