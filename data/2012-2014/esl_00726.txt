There is the 2006 statistics of passenger transportation at different international airports and their position according to number of passengers, which shows some interesting facts.
Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport, the top ranked airport, saved its position between 2005 and 2006, but lost 1.2%. 
The same situation happened to O’Hare International Airport (ranked 2nd), which also maintained its position taking more than 76 million passengers and lost just 0.3%.
Similarly, London Heathrow Airport didn’t move from the 3rd position with 67530223 passengers, but the same to Hartsfield-Jackson International Airport and O’Hare International Airport it lost 0.6% from 2005.
On the other hand, Paris Charles De Gaulle Airport (ranked 7th) and Suvarnabhumi Airport (ranked 15th) increased their positions.  Paris Charles De Gaulle Airport transported 56 million passengers, an increase of 5.6%.  And Suvarnabhumi Airport transported over 42 million passengers, an increase a great 9.8%.
There was a great increase in passenger traffic at Beijing International Airport (ranked 9th). It increased the volume by a fascinating 18.3% between 2005 and 2006.
