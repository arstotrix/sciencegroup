Nowadays is the time of market relation all huge companies or themajor  players of market try to get and retrain the most professional specialist. It is impossible to retain staff only  money, therefore, companies are looking for alternative ways. For this reasonthe attention is devoted to form organizational trust, which contains institute trust, impersonal trust, trust to manager. This type of trust is essential for  formation of organizational identify. The stuff with high level of organizational identify achieve more, are more successful in their career, work  more harder, and do not leave the company during the crisis. 
This paper is reviews the independence between organizational trust and organization identify. Moreover this paper is shows the process of formation of organization identify and its role in   building of business process.
