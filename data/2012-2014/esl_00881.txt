Report on Herbal Medicines
Introduction
This report investigates herbal medicines and considers whether the therapy is effective and safe. The report gathered information from the following sources:
-	4,500 members surveyed (2,815 completed / sent back)
-	Articles from well-known medical journals

Facts and findings
1.	Herbal medicines use plant extracts for remedies/medicines
2.	Herbs have been used for thousands of years – ancient remedy
3.	Three types of medicines: western, Chinese, Indian
4.	Safety: users need to take care. Side effects from some herbs. Also, they can interact badly whit other drugs
5.	Members found herbs very effective for a wide range of illness; 55 percent would recommend herbal medicine to friends

Conclusion
Some herbal medicines have a harmful effect on the body.
But many benefits from herbs as well.

Recommendations
1.	Must be used with great care – get advice from a herbalist
2.	Absolutely essential to tell your doctor if you are using herbal medicine
3.	Make sure the herbal remedy is the correct product for you
4.	Good idea for a herbalist to make a remedy specially for you
5.	Don’t believe all the claims you read on the packing of a herbal product
