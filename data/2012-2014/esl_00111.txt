In modern society, the major part of people give preference totheir professional life minimized other aspects of it. Thus the process of a professional choice and its subsequent changes becomes important for people and as a result for their fullfill life. Accordingly, people's attitude to their professional life is studied in psychology. In this paper this question will be considered within the framework of existential theory. More precisely the reasons that a person based on in changing attitudes towards the professional life will be examined.
