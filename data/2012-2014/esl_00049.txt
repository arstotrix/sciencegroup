Psychological functions of hope.
Hope  -  is one of the most important  human spiritual properties, which helps to cope with life's difficulties.  In our daily lives it has many functions, which may seem to be not significant enough to be become an object of  research. The results of a few researches of this topic identify basically the positive functions of hope. Nevertheless sometimes hope may have bad influence on one's life, and this property of hope remains to be unexplored. 
Thus in the first instant the goal of  course paper is to explore the impact of hope on one's life and decisions in all its manifestations, and secondarily to pick out the negative functions of hope and investigate them more carefully.
