Tendencies in the development of a modern family
It is commonly assumed  that nowadays the institution of family is undergoing a deep crisis. There is a considerable amount of a research which portrays family  a dying structure. The current paper attempts to prove the opposite view. The alleged manifestations of the family crisis are interpreted in this research as signs of the family's adaptation and self-regulation as  system (as proposed in System Family Therapy). The main consequences of adaptation are variability of family's forms and psychological "benefits" for each person in  family alliance. This point will be approving  by interview members of different  Russian families
