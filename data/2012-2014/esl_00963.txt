Nowadays there are a lot of companies all over the world. Some of them are very successful and others are not. Does the success depend on the friendly atmosphere and happy employees?
Some people think that happiness of workers can help company to become successful. They think so because people who like doing their job can do their tasks more carefully and more effectively. Also, they will be willing to do more things that they should do.  So, this can make a work in a company more effective and later this company will probably become a successful one.
However, there are people who think that the success of the company only depends on how employees are good in their field of work and how professionally they can handle tasks.
