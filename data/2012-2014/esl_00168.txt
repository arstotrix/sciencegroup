Self-reflection as the source of changing stimulating value of attraction object 
Reflection, or a human ability of internal cognition, is not only a cognitive ability of soul. This study, however, proposes that reflection is more than merely thinking about existing motives, desires, abilities; it can actually influence their emergence. By reflecting on some desire, people bring it into being. The research concentrates on how reflection can influence on power of the particular phenomenon - temptation. The hypotheses of research are the following: 1. the awareness or temptation can influence its strength; 2. this awareness intensifies rather than reduces it.
