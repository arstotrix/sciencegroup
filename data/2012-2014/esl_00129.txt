 Since antiquity until now, there is a lot of attention for forming and development capabilities pose particular interesting for learning.
 For example, Aristotel shows in his own works  correlation between general and spiritual capabilities, and  influence of it on  person's activity. Buttoday, modern society focus on social achievements in   exclusion of development of spiritual capabilities, in particular, the capability of good deeds. This focus can lead to serious deficit in personal spiritual development. In order to encourage spiritual capabilities, it is important to understand how they forming and mature in a person. Therefore, this work consider decide next tasks:  review   main foreign and domestic theories of   person's capabilities;  define place of spiritual capabilities in the theory of V.D. Shadrikov;  allocate   factors of maturation of spiritual capabilities;  defineplace and sense of spiritual capabilities in  person's life.
