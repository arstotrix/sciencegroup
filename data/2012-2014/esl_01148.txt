It is known that sports, which embroil animals, have always been widely popular. Some people claim that those kinds of sports should be restricted, whereas others think that there is no need in banning them. I share the first opinion.
There are some arguments supporting my point of view. First of all, it is quite obvious that those animals involved into sports are always under the threat of being badly hurt. If it happens, many of them receive no proper treatment. Moreover, apart from the existence of such legal sports like horse-riding or polo, there are many illegal ones. For instance, dog fighting. Those include even drugging animals so that they could continue participating.
