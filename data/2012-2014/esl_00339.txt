Words 'dry' and 'wet' and their synonyms in English.
Synonyms are useful words to increase an expressiveness of speech and they help to avoid the monotony of it. But sometimes it is hard to understand the difference between words and choose the right word in the context. For "Words 'dry' and 'wet' and their synonyms in English" course-paper motivation comes from the fact that there are many synonyms of words 'wet' and 'dry' and there is a small amount of  dictionaries that can provide information on semantic and collocational differences among synonyms. However, only a few words are presented in dictionary's article although there are more synonyms of the word. For this course-paper, long-term goal is to create a database with these synonyms, their definition and with explanation of difference in meaning. Moreover, this database can give an answer, for instance, why the words 'wet' and ' aqueous' cannot be used in the one context.
