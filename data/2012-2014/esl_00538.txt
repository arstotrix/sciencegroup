The places visited by different people in Australia. 
In this research we can see quantity of people, which prefer to visit different places. People are broke up into the groups: persons, who born in Australia, new migrants, which born in English-speaking countries and new migrants, which born in other countries at last.
Researches analyze attendance fours public places: zoo, library, theatre and cinema.
At this diagram we can notice that theatre is the most unpopular  kind of pastime and this place attend basically emigrants with native  English.
The third position takes a Library and Zoo. But Library more popular by migrants from other countries.
And the most popular public place is the cinema.
