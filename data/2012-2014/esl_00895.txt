According to the summary table, we can see that the level of women who have the only one child has grown up from the 1981. But the level of women who have four and more children has sharply decreased. It is easy to see that the percentage of women who have three children has not changed greatly in this period of time. The level of women who has one or two children has changed a lot compared to 1981.It is interesting, that the changes in percentage of women who have no children or have the only one child have similarly decreased.
