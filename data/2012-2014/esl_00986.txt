Report on Herbal medicines
Introduction
This report investigates herbal medicines and considers whether these medicines are safe and effective.
The report gathered information from the following sources:
•	4500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using herbal medicines. 2815 of the surveys were successfully completed
•	Articles from well-known medical journals
    Facts 
1.	Plant extracts are used for remedies
2.	Herbal remedies are ancient: they have been used for thousands of years
3.	There are three types of herbal medicines: western, Chinese and Indian
4.	You need to take care while using herbal remedies: there may be effects from some herbs and also they interact badly with other drugs
5.	55% of our members found herbs very effective for a wide range of illnesses and would recommend them to friends
Conclusion
The results of our survey are controversial. On one hand, some herbal medicines have a harmful effect on the body. On the other hand, there are many benefits from herbs as well.
Recommendations
•	Herbal remedies must be used with great care – you should get advice from a herbalist beforehand
•	It is absolutely essential to tell your doctor if you are using herbal medicine
•	You should make sure if the herbal remedy is the correct product for you
•	It is a good idea for a herbalist to make a remedy specially for you
•	You should not believe all the claims you read on the packaging of a herbal product
