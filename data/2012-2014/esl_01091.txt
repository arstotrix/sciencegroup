Report on Herbal medicines
Introduction 
This report investigates herbal medicines 
The report gathered information from the following sources:
•	4,500 of our members were sent questionnaires asking them about their experience of using medicine. We received 2,815 completed surveys.
•	Articles from leading medical journals.
Facts and findings
1.	Illnesses are treated  with plant extracts.
2.	Herbs was developed in ancient time. And have been used for thousands of years.
3.	There are three types of herbal medicines: western, Chinese, Indian.
4.	Herbs is considered not so safe by most experts. Users need to take care, because of side effects and herbs can interact badly with other drugs.
5.	Our members used herbal medicines to treat a wide range of illnesses. Just over 55% said that they would recommend the treatment to friends.
Conclusion
Scientific experts and users of the therapy claim that some herbs have a harmful effect on the body. However, there are many benefits from herbs as well.
Recommendations
1.	You should get advise from a herbalist before using herbs remedies.
2.	It is vital to tell your doctor about using herbal medicine.
3.	It is advisable to make sure that the herbal remedy is the correct product for you.
4.	It is a good idea for herbalist to make a remedy specially for you.
5.	You should not believe all the claims you read on the packaging of a herbal product.
