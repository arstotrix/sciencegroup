Colour perceive on different levels including emotional. Emotions provoked by color are named colour emotions. Color has 3 appearance attributes: hue, light and chroma. This study defines  connection between colour emotions and colour appearance attributes.In earlier works researcher defined similar factors which were used in different studies on different cultures. This study investigates this colour-emotional scales, comparisons were made between male/female, British/Chinese groups.
To categorize colour emotions, were used factor analyses. Investigation has shown that there are universal color-emotional factors which were similar accessed by observers, it's colour activity colour weight and colour heat.
