Report on herbal remedies
Introduction
The purpose of this report is to investigate plant-based treatments and to find out whether they are effective and safe. 
This report gathered information from the following sources:
- 4500 of our members were surveyed on their experience of using these treatments and answered questionnaires. We received 2,815 completed questionnaires.
- We also used the information from well-known medical journals
Facts and findings
1. For producing herbal remedies plant extracts are used. The medicine can be based on flowers, berries, bark and many other natural components.
2. Herbs are an ancient remedy. They have been used for thousands of years.
3. There are three types of the medicines in the world: western, Chinese and Indian. They are different and people can choose which one they like the most.
4. For their own safety users should be very careful in trying these remedies because side effects from some herbs can take place. Also, they can interact badly with other drugs.
5. Our members found herbal medicines very effective for a wide range of illness. 55 percent said that they would recommend these treatments to friends.
Conclusion
In the light of the result of the survey there are many benefits from herbal medicines. However, scientific experts say that some of them have a harmful effect on the body.
Recommendations
1. Herbal medicines must be used with a great care! Before using them you should get advice from a herbalist.
2. If you are using these treatments, it is absolutely essential to tell your doctor about this fact.
3. Make sure the herbal remedy is the correct product for you.
4. Asking a herbalist to make a remedy specially for you will be a good idea.
5. You should not believe all the claims you read on the packaging of a herbal product.
